Services

What we offer

Preventative Care

Preventive care includes - Vaccinations, fecal analysism annual exams, spay, neutering, dental cleaning, and wellness blood work.

Surgery

We offer a full range of surgical procedures such as spay/neuter, laser and regular declaws, foreign body, bladder surgery, soft tissue surgeries such as lump removals.

Urgent Care

We are open everyday, even during Sundays, with convenient hours including evenings for all of your pets urgent care needs. You can just walk-in to our facility for anything from a cut, strain or sprain to a more serious matter.

Complete In-House Lab

Full service in house bloodwork to include heartworm, lyme disease and all other tick born diseases. For the cat in your life we can also offer Feline Leukemia and FIV testing to make sure all is well. We can do more extensive testing such as liver and kidney panel and thyroid testing plus much more and this we send out to the lab.

General Routine Care

Walk-in with your pet for some vaccinations that include a free exam. We can also trim up your pet's nails for you and if needed the clinic can provide you a healt certificate, both local and international. We have a new state of the art digital X-Ray stsem that allowes use to access the image right away. Prescription food is also available to you in stock and we can also create a special orders for special needs.

Heartworm and Lyme Testing

We offer complete testing/prevention and treatment of Heartworm disease, Lyme disease, and other tick borne diseases.
